This project is an Android app called "One-Platform" which is a platform facilities the university life of students. The platform has mainly two functions: one is 
called "Anonymous Social-Platform" which is a anonymous platform that students can post text on it and other users can see it, like it, comment it but can not
see who is the poster. The another one is called "Job-platform" which students can post his willing to looking for a job or for employer to post 
offers and requirements.
